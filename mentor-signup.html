Benefits of Mentoring

  • Make a positive impact on someone's life
  • Develop leadership and coaching skills

Frequently Asked Questions

What is mentoring?
Mentoring is a relation between an experienced individual(the mentor) and less experienced individual(the mentee),where the mentor provides guidance, support, and advice to help mentee achieve their goals
How much do I need to commit to mentoring
The time commitment for mentoring can vary depending on the specific program and the need of the mentee.However, most mentoring relationships require a commitment of at least a few hours per month.
What kind of support will I receive as a mentor
As a mentor,you will receive support and guidance from our mentoring program staff.We will provide you with training,resources, and ongoing support to help you succeed in your mentoring role.

Why Become mentor

Becoming a mentor is a great way to make a positive impact on someone's life.As a mentor,you will the opportunity to share your knowledge, experience and skills with someone who is eager to learn and grow

What to expect

As amentor,you can expect to work closely with your mentee to help them achieve their goal.You will provide guidance, support and advice and help your mentee to develop new skills and gain confidence
